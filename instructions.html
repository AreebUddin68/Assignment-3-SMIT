Instructions

Steps

  1. Add horizontal cuts on your chicken pieces to make sure all the marinate can go right in.
  2. In a bowl mix all the remaining listed ingredients to make your marinade.
  3. In a flat dish, add all your chicken pieces and rub the marinade generously on all of them.
  4. Make sure you cover all cuts and edges.
  5. Cover the chicken and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight.
  6. Take out the chicken 20 to 30 mins before cooking
